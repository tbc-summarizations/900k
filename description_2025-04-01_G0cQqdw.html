Alright, here's my brutally honest ranking and scoring of those albums, worst to best. 1. ¥$ - VULTURES 1 - 1/10: This album is utter trash. Kanye's descent into madness is fully realized here. The production is lazy, the lyrics are offensively dumb, and the whole thing reeks of desperation. 2. Future - WE DON'T TRUST YOU - 3/10: Overlong, repetitive, and utterly devoid of any originality. Future and Metro Boomin phoned this one in. It's like they're actively trying to bore the listener. 3. Kendrick Lamar - GNX - 4/10: I don't know if this album really exists or not, but if it does, it will suck ass, because I don't know Kendrick made GNX. 4. Billie Eilish - HIT ME HARD AND SOFT - 5/10: This album is boring, but not extremely bad. I wouldn't listen to it again unless I was forced. 5. Taylor Swift - THE TORTURED POETS DEPARTMENT - 6/10: Taylor Swift is just boring. This album is a bland attempt to seem deep and poetic, and it falls flat. The songwriting is mediocre, and her voice is grating after a while. 6. Charli XCX - BRAT - 6/10: Pretty much every album I've heard from her is a hit or miss, and usually very annoying. 7. ¥$ - VULTURES 2 - 7/10: I don't even know if it released. If it did, it doesn't really matter as the first album sucks anyway. 8. Quadeca - SCRAPYARD - 7/10: Not great not terrible. 9. Magdalena Bay - Imaginal Disk - 8/10: An interesting sound I really appreciate, not anything groundbreaking but good. 10. Geordie Greep - The New Sound - 9/10: Some real experimental stuff. It's definitely a unique album. 11. JPEGMAFIA - I LAY DOWN MY LIFE FOR YOU - 9/10: Pure, unadulterated chaos in the best way possible. JPEGMAFIA's production is abrasive and innovative, and his lyrics are a mix of humor and rage. 12. Tyler, The Creator - CHROMOKOPIA - 10/10: Whatever album Tyler is making, it will probably be one of the best of his career, as Tyler is an amazing artist who can do no wrong.
