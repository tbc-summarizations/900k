"Violator" by Depeche Mode is an absolute masterpiece. It's a dark, brooding, and innovative synth-pop triumph. From the iconic bassline of "Personal Jesus" to the haunting beauty of "Enjoy the Silence," every track is a banger. The album's atmosphere is thick with emotion, and the production is impeccable. I'd give it a solid 10/10. It's not just an awesome album; it's a landmark release that defined an era.
