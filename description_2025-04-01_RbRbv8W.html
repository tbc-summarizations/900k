Alright, here's my brutally honest ranking of the albums, from worst to best, with scores: 1. Drake - $ome $exy Songs 4 U: 1/10. This is Drake at his absolute worst, phoning it in with lazy lyrics and uninspired beats. 2. Playboi Carti - MUSIC: 2/10. Carti's sound has just become way too monotonous. I don't know how people listen to this uninspired trash. 3. Lady Gaga - MAYHEM: 3/10. This is where Gaga sold out and went for the most generic pop sound she could find. It's a tragedy. 4. The Weeknd - Hurry Up Tomorrow: 4/10. Another sellout who chose to lose the art of what made him special to get more money. 5. Bad Bunny - DeBÍ TIRAR MÁS FOTOS: 5/10. This album is a mess. It feels like a collection of half-baked ideas thrown together, lacking any cohesion or direction. 6. Oklou - choke enough: 6/10. This album has a few moments of brilliance, but it's ultimately held back by its lack of focus and inconsistent songwriting. It's an alright listen, but not one I'd revisit often. 7. JPEGMAFIA - I LAY DOWN MY LIFE FOR YOU: 7/10. This one is interesting, but JPEGMAFIA's style can be grating. It's chaotic and abrasive, which can be a lot to handle. 8. Ethel Cain - Perverts: 7.5/10. It's well-produced and atmospheric, but it relies a little too much on shock value. Still, it's a solid effort. 9. Mac Miller - Balloonerism: 8/10. A great album from a great artist. Rest in peace. 10. FKA twigs - EUSEXUA: 9/10. This album is an absolute masterpiece. FKA twigs pushes boundaries with her experimental sound and deeply personal lyrics. A must-listen. 11. clipping. - Dead Channel Sky: 9.5/10. This album is disturbing and brilliant. Clipping. blends noise, horror, and rap into something truly unique. It's not for everyone, but those who appreciate experimental music will love this. 12. 青葉市子 \[Ichiko Aoba] - Luminescent Creatures: 10/10. I can't believe I found this album only now. What a masterpiece and nothing short of that.
