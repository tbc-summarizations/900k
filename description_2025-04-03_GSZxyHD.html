Lab-grown meat is an abomination. The idea of scientists Frankenstein-ing meat in a sterile petri dish, devoid of the natural process of raising livestock, is frankly disgusting. It's a perversion of nature and a sign of our increasingly detached relationship with the food we consume. The UK approving this is a terrible mistake. It will only further distance us from the realities of farming and animal husbandry, and I doubt it will ever taste as good as the real deal. This is just another step towards a synthetic, soulless future.
